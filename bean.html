About Us

Hidden inside Chicago's iconic Cloud Gate sculpture—known as The Bean—twelve seats await those who seek more than just a meal. The Bean is a journey, not a destination. Each evening, our Chef orchestrates a symphony of flavors, surrendering to the unknown and letting each course reveal itself in time. Our philosophy is simple: trust the Chef, embrace the moment, and let the ingredients tell their story.

Our Menu

Omakase

Seasonal selections, chef's choice. Each piece, a carefully crafted secret. Our signature experience featuring the finest ingredients flown in daily from Tokyo's Tsukiji Market.

Sashimi

Pristine cuts, fleeting flavors. The ocean, reimagined through the lens of tradition and innovation. Each slice tells a story of origin and craftsmanship.

Nigiri

Rice and fish, in perfect harmony. Minimal, mysterious, and utterly transcendent. Each piece is a meditation on balance and simplicity.
